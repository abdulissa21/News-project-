Who we are, and what we do

Leading the Car Remapping Industry in the UK Narkos Performance stands as the top choice for vehicle tuning in Greater Manchester.
With years of expertise in remapping various vehicle models, our specialty lies in engine tuning and remapping for optimal performance and fuel economy.
Bored of your drive? Remap your car with Narkos Performance today!

ECU READING & DIAGNOSTICS
At Narkos Performance, we provide bespoke remapped tuning files, created live on the spot during your visit. Our process begins with a thorough diagnostic check
to identify any potential major fault codes in the vehicle that could disrupt the ECU writing process. Live Datalogging of your vehicle may take place.

EXTRACTING YOUR ORIGINAL ECU OR TCU FILE
We utilize Genuine Autotuner, Kess V3, Flex, and other reputable tools to extract the original file from the vehicle's Electronic Control Unit (ECU). Our process ensures 100% authenticity, employing genuine hardware and software for both ECU reading and writing.

FILE MODIFICATION AND ENHANCING
Ultimately, we meticulously enhance the ECU file and seamlessly rewrite it into your vehicle, resulting in improved performance. Our collaboration extends to WinOLS authorized resellers and dealers, ensuring a trusted and authorized process throughout. Warranty over software available.
